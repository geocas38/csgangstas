guideme

by CS Gangstaz

guideme is a fast, comprehensive, and intuitive trip planner that scales to everyone needs. Simply inputting the trip parameters presents a list of the best matched places in the city. Powered by Yelp, guideme removes the hassle of planning trips, allowing every traveller to enjoy their trip the way it was meant to be enjoyed. Log into the future of travelling and explore more of the world. Accessible, convenient, and innovative.
